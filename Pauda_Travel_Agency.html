The Sultans Palace, New Orleans, Lousiana

Why you should go to the Sultan's Palace?

If your looking for a sfun time with friends and family and have enough guts to go to one of the most haunted locations in the United States then you should visit The Sultan's Palace. For almost 200 years legend has it that a Turkish man that claimed to be the so called "Sultan" bought and moved into the house. One afternoon a man noticied blood seeping out of the door of the Sultans house and called the police. The police discovered a 27 person mass murder killing adults and children including the sultan himself. The murderer was never found and the palace is supposedly haunted with the victims spirits. So if your up for a scary night up adventure the Sultan's Palace is for you....
